About me

I work at Orange Labs R&D, Issy-les-Moulineaux as "Ingenieur Recherche et Developpement" [English : R&D Engineer] and as Postdoctoral researcher for the ANR ABCD Project. Before this position, I was a PhD candidate at CITI Research Laboratory, Lyon France. I was involved in INRIA UrbaNet team with Dr Marco Fiore and Prof. Fabrice Valois and was a member of the Alcatel Lucent Bell Labs - INRIA common lab. I also worked as Early Stage Researcher (ESR) at FORTH, Herklion, Greece with Prof. Angelos Bilas and Dr. Michail Flouris.
